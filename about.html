About Me

Sophie Christoforou (27th September, 2003), known professionally as LostGem is a British singer from Croydon, United Kingdom. Known for her distinct vocal style and emotive lyrics, LostGem began gaining attention in the UK music scene in early 2024. She released her debut single, "Perfect," on 27 September 2024, in collaboration with British rapper Ryze Tha Kidd. The single marked the beginning of her professional music career, showcasing her unique blend of Trap, Pop and R&B influences. LostGem continues to build her reputation as an emerging artist in the British music industry, captivating audiences with her soulful voice and compelling performances.
